THE INTERVIEW OF REDEMPTION - Roswit A

A Story of Rivalry, Redemption & Forgiveness

Imagine meeting your childhood friend after many years. It would be a moment filled with excitement, awkwardness, and a flood of forgotten memories. About The Book

Summary

Jake Warrison has built the life he always dreamed of—a successful career, a loving family, and a respected reputation. During an ordinary job interview, he unexpectedly comes face to face with someone from his past, forcing him to revisit painful memories he believed were long forgotten. As old wounds resurface and hidden truths come to light, both men are faced with choices that could change their lives forever.
